Natural laws, from first principles

Every law of nature rests on a simpler one beneath it. Logic lets us think. Numbers let us count. Calculus lets us describe change. Symmetry gives us conservation. Conservation gives us Newton. Newton gives us orbits and Einstein. And on, and on — all the way up to stars, to life, to minds.

This is the map. Foundations at the centre. Each ring outward is built from the one inside. Each colour is a different branch of knowledge.

Click any node for an explanation a 5-year-old could follow, a deeper one for grown-ups, the equation, and a live interactive simulation you can play with.
